Proposal: Husks hold sand

This would make husk zombies occasionally hold sand, so that a mob farm built in a desert can automate the production of sand. Husks can only spawn on the surface of deserts, and there will likely be a low probability of 1/64 to avoid it being overpowered.

Because this may impact the economy, we will put a supermajority vote threshold with a requirement to exceed the percentage of the previous vote.
